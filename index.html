UNEP Committee

Model United Nations Conference

1. Introduction

The United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) Committee in a Model United Nations (MUN) setting simulates the work of the UNEA and engages delegates in multilateral environmental policymaking.

2. Mandate & Objectives

  • Environmental Assessment: Gather and analyze data on global environmental trends.
  • Policy Formulation: Develop international guidelines and agreements.
  • Capacity-Building: Advocate for technology transfer and financial assistance.
  • Scientific Cooperation: Ensure policies are backed by the latest research.

3. Committee Structure

  • Membership: All UN Member States.
  • Dais: Chair, Deputy Chair(s).

4. Typical Session Flow

  1. Roll Call & Agenda Setting
  2. General Debate
  3. Caucusing: Moderated & Unmoderated caucuses.
  4. Drafting Resolutions

5. Draft Resolution Components

  • Preambulatory Clauses: Contextual statements citing past agreements.
  • Operative Clauses: Specific actions, timelines, and funding.
